Goals

The primary goal of the website is to get more people to buy tickets for the Barrier Reef Orchestra. Secondary goals include getting more members to sign up and support the local Orchestra, getting local artists and conductors to get involved in the production of shows, and to get younger people into the Orchestra.

Success Evaluation

The Success of the website will be evaluated by wheather or not more people buy tickets for Barrier Reef Orchestra, and if more local artist show interest in joining the Orchestra.

Target Audience

The Target Audience for the Barrier Reef Orchestra is generally people of all ages, though the website should be aiming towards getting a younger audience involved (age 12-25)
